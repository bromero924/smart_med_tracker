Med Tracker

Med Tracker is a medication management platform that helps users stay on track with their prescriptions.

How it works

  • Users create a medication schedule
  • Receive reminders before doses
  • Receive alerts if a dose is missed
  • Track medication history

SMS Notifications

By providing your phone number and registering in the app, you agree to receive SMS notifications related to:

  • Medication reminders
  • Dose confirmations
  • Missed dose alerts
  • Critical health alerts

Message frequency varies depending on your medication schedule.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

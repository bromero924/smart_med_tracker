Privacy Policy

Med Tracker collects user information such as name, email address, and phone number for the purpose of providing medication reminders, dose confirmations, missed dose alerts, and critical health notifications.

Phone numbers are used only for transactional SMS notifications related to medication management. We do not sell or share user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Users may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP. Users may reply HELP for assistance.
